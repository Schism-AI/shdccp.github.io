$$U(\theta) = e^{-i \sigma_z \theta / 2}$$

where $\sigma_z$ is the Pauli spin matrix.

For a rotation of $\theta = 2\pi$ (360 degrees):

$$U(2\pi) = e^{-i \sigma_z \pi} = \cos(\pi)I - i\sin(\pi)\sigma_z = -I$$

$$U(4\pi) = e^{-i \sigma_z 2\pi} = \cos(2\pi)I - i\sin(2\pi)\sigma_z = I$$
